The Area

Garland, TX —
more than a layover.

Garland is a vibrant DFW suburb with a relaxed community feel. Historic Downtown Garland (4 mi) offers local dining, live music at the Plaza Theatre, and boutique shops. Rowlett Creek Preserve (2.5 mi) has 97 acres of trails and fishing. Lake Ray Hubbard (5 mi) offers boating and lakeside dining. Firewheel Golf Park (6 mi) has 63 holes across three courses. Dallas landmarks — the Arboretum, Deep Ellum, and Uptown — are 15–18 miles away.
